for Mom & Baby

It’s not your same-old pregnancy class. In fact, it’s not a class at all. It’s a community of moms that share support and real-world guidance.

Wellhop for Mom & Baby uses virtual group conversations and fun (sometimes funny) educational tools to help women get ready for their new baby.

With Wellhop for Mom & Baby you can:

  • Talk to other moms at the same stage in pregnancy.
  • Get the latest information on pregnancy and postpartum health.
  • Join conversations from anywhere - work, home, etc.
  • Build lasting relationships with other moms as you journey through pregnancy and postpartum together.

Wellhop™ for Mom & Baby is offered through your employer-sponsored health plan. Contact your HR department about other benefits.

Currently, Wellhop for Mom & Baby is offered exclusively to Sephora, Target, UnitedHealth Group, and Wells Fargo employees and their dependents covered by UnitedHealthcare Medical Plan.

This program is available at no cost for all eligible participants.

How It Works:

Virtual Community

Wellhop for Mom & Baby brings women with similar due dates together to share experiences, learn together, and support each other. You meet with the same women each time and these hour long conversations are led by a trained facilitator.

The group meets every other week for a total of 13 prenatal sessions and 6 postpartum sessions (about 9 months).

You might be thinking…

“I have a prenatal care provider, the internet and a ton of apps. Why would I join Wellhop for Mom & Baby?”
